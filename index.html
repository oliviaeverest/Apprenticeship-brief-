WHY IT EXISTS

Built from one route through the process — not a general theory of it.

Most advice on this route is either a firm's own marketing or a forum thread. This is neither.

01

Tested, not theorised

Every template and note comes from an application that was actually submitted and an assessment centre that was actually sat.

02

Anonymised by design

Firm-specific material is generalised into patterns you can apply anywhere, rather than answers tied to one process.

03

Built to use, not admire

Editable templates and a live tracker, not a static document you read once and close.
